========================================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
========================================
Holistic Lakewood is a wellness center located in Lakewood, Ohio (just west of Cleveland) that offers a variety of holistic health services including Massage Therapy, Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD), Reiki, Reflexology, Aromatherapy, Biomat Therapy, and ionic detox footbath.

The center opened its doors at the end of 2011, offering wellness solutions to those seeking a more holistic approach to their health and well-being. They focus on helping people suffering with physical pain, stress, anxiety, and more.

Services are by appointment only. Hours: Monday-Thursday 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM, Friday 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Saturday-Sunday 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM.

Website: holisticlakewood.com
