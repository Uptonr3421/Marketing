====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
Studio West 117 is a continually evolving neighborhood of LGBTQ+ safe spaces bridging the gap between Lakewood and Cleveland, Ohio. Spanning over 300,000 square feet, it's an evolving collection of entertainment, sports, and dining venues in addition to housing, identity-affirming businesses, and social services. The project represents a $115-125 million investment developed by Daniel Budish and Betsy Figgie to become a hub of entrepreneurship, arts, culture, health, and human services for Greater Cleveland's LGBTQ+ community.

Phase 1 - The Fieldhouse: 24,000 sq ft community hub with restaurant, pizza kitchen, rooftop bar, gymnasium, and outdoor courtyard
Future: Phantasy Entertainment Complex (11802 Detroit Avenue) with six themed entertainment areas focused on LGBTQ nightlife and elevating drag art
Additional planned spaces: Podcast studio, maker space, coffee shop, mental health services
Website: studiowest117.com
