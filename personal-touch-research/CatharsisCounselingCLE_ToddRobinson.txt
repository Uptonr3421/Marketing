BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
===========================================
Catharsis Counseling CLE, LLC is a private mental health practice in the Greater Cleveland area (Beachwood/Pepper Pike) specializing in working with teenagers, mothers, couples, and members of the LGBTQ+ community.

Services & Specializations:
- Individual therapy for teenagers and adults
- Couples counseling
- LGBTQ+ affirming care and gender affirming support
- Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
- Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)
- Trauma-informed care and trauma recovery
- Eating disorder treatment
- Sex therapy (sex positive, kink positive approach)
- Mindfulness-based interventions
- Evidence-based treatments

Issues Treated:
- Depression
- Anxiety
- Life stressors
- Self-harm
- Relationship issues
- LGBTQ+ identity exploration
- Trauma and PTSD
- Eating disorders

The practice creates a collaborative relationship within a non-judgmental environment and is dedicated to helping clients make lasting changes that lead to a happier and fuller life.
