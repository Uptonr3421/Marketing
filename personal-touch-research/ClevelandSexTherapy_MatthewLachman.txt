BUSINESS DESCRIPTION:
Cleveland Sex Therapy is a specialized therapy practice focused exclusively on sex therapy, founded by Matthew Lachman in 2019. The practice provides individual and relationship therapy using queer and feminist approaches. Their mission is to promote sex positivity and inclusivity, with clinicians who are poly, kink, and queer-affirming. Located at 6516 Detroit Ave. Suite 3, Cleveland, OH 44102.

FOUNDER BACKGROUND:
Matthew Lachman (he/him) is a Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor-Supervisor (LPCC-S) and AASECT Certified Sex Therapist-Supervisor (CST-S). He became a certified sex therapist through AASECT in November 2021 and has been focusing solely on sex therapy since 2018. He is a certified polyamorous specialist with expertise in queer theory, polyamory, and kink. Reviews describe him as knowledgeable and compassionate.
