BUSINESS DESCRIPTION:
Compass Policy Strategies LLC is an independent, woman-owned consultancy focused on workforce development, education systems, clean technology, and regional economy. Since 2015, the firm has been helping clients plan, implement, and sustain sector strategies and industry sector partnerships within the workforce development space. Areas of expertise include multi-stakeholder initiatives, collaboratives, and strategic planning processes.

FOUNDER BACKGROUND:
Janine Spadafore Kaiser is the Principal of Compass Policy Strategies, which she has led since 2015. She has nearly 20 years of experience designing and leading multi-stakeholder initiatives, collaboratives, and strategic planning processes in workforce, education, and economic development. Her experience includes supporting partnerships in Manufacturing, Information Technology, Health, Advanced Transportation, and Hospitality sectors across California, Arizona, Pennsylvania, Ohio, and Nevada. She launched the firm in the San Francisco Bay Area and later relocated it to Greater Cleveland.
