=== BUSINESS DESCRIPTION ===
In Good Company Jewelry is a handcrafted jewelry business owned and operated by Tess Pell, a Cleveland-based goldsmith who creates custom fine jewelry in her home studio. Tess designs and handmakes every piece using traditional metalsmithing techniques including hand sawing, soldering, forging, rolling, enameling, and stone setting.

Services & Products:
- Custom fine jewelry design
- Alternative fine jewelry (her true passion)
- Traditional fine jewelry pieces
- Solid precious metals (gold, silver)
- Natural gemstones and diamonds
- Ethically sourced materials
- Personalized custom orders
