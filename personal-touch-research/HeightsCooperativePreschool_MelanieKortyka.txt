========================================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
========================================
Heights Cooperative Preschool (formerly St. Paul's Cooperative) is a small, community-funded preschool serving Cleveland Heights, Ohio. The school has been teaching preschoolers in the Heights area for over 50 years, utilizing play-based learning and emergent curriculum to inspire a love of learning for preschool and Pre-K students.

Located inside the Church of the Redeemer United Methodist Church at 2420 S. Taylor Road, Cleveland Heights, Ohio 44118, Heights Cooperative offers cooperative preschool, preschool, pre-kindergarten, toddler classes and early learning classes.

Classrooms are designed to allow children to choose activities and self-direct with learning centers and interest-based activities including dramatic play, open-ended art projects, investigation activities, circle discussions, and math games.

Website: heightscooppreschool.org

========================================
PAIN POINTS
========================================

1. **Enrollment Competition**: Cleveland Heights area has many preschool options (Fairmount Coop, church programs, Montessori, corporate chains). Standing out requires clear differentiation and marketing.

2. **Parent Awareness**: Families new to Cleveland Heights may not know about Heights Coop or understand cooperative preschool model. Requires consistent outreach to expectant parents and families with toddlers.

3. **Explaining Cooperative Model**: Cooperative preschool model (parent participation, shared governance) is less common than traditional preschool. Requires education about benefits and expectations.

4. **Facility Dependence**: Operating inside church building means dependent on church relationship and facility availability. May limit expansion or scheduling flexibility.

5. **Digital Presence**: Small, community-funded organization may have limited marketing budget and capacity for sophisticated website, social media, and digital marketing.
