BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
Oasis Counseling Solutions LLC provides behavioral health therapy in Strongsville and Lakewood, Ohio, offering both in-person and telehealth appointments. The practice is run by owner Mondie Gonzales, LISW-S (Licensed Independent Social Worker-Supervisor), who has worked with kids and families in community mental health for most of her 30-year career. She opened private practice 10 years ago, working with individuals, couples, and families. The practice has multiple clinicians on staff including Emily and Erin. Mondie has an eclectic approach with focus on trauma-informed care and healthy living. The practice philosophy believes clients are experts in their own lives, with clinicians offering support and guidance to meet client goals.
