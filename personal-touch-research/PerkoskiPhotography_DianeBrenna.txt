====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
Perkoski Photography is a creative and versatile freelance photography business in Cleveland specializing in media, events, architectural/real estate photography, and more. Bob Perkoski is a highly accomplished Cleveland photographer who served as FreshWater Cleveland's managing photographer from 2010-2024, creating the beloved #StreetsofCLE series capturing everyday life in Cleveland neighborhoods. He is the official photographer for LAND Studio, Holden Forests & Gardens, and numerous Cleveland arts organizations including LatinUs Theater, BorderLight Fringe Festival, MorrisonDance, The Nature Center at Shaker Lakes, and Collaborative to End Human Trafficking. His work has won multiple Press Club of Cleveland journalism awards.

Portfolio: Photojournalism, events, theater, dance, music & entertainment, people/environmental portraits, architectural/real estate, industrial, race events, fine art, Cleveland street photography
Published works: "Rust Belt Burlesque" (2019), "LGBTQ Cleveland" by Ken Schneck, "Rust Belt Chic: The Cleveland Anthology," "Rust Belt Chic: The Pittsburgh Anthology," "Moon Cleveland" by Douglas Trattner
