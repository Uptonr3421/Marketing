========================================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
========================================
Emma Parker Photography is a woman-owned, LGBTQIA+ photographer based in Columbus, Ohio (not Cleveland). Emma started her photography business in 2012 after backpacking through Europe. She offers multiple photography services including family portraiture, professional portraits, headshots/personal branding, senior portraits, event photography, wedding photography, and commercial photography.

Emma is WBE, EDGE, LGBTBE, and DRONE Certified, demonstrating her commitment to professional excellence and diversity certification.

========================================
RECENT WINS/NEWS/ACHIEVEMENTS
========================================

1. **Trans Visibility Project (2021-present)**: Emma launched this groundbreaking project in 2021 to create a platform and safe space for the transgender and non-binary community to share their visual identity stories. She has photographed close to 40 people with a goal of 100. This project has been featured in multiple exhibitions.

2. **Stonewall Columbus Leader of Pride 2022**: Emma was recognized as a 2022 Stonewall Columbus Leader of Pride for her Trans Visibility Project work.

3. **Major Gallery Exhibitions**: The Trans Visibility Project has been displayed at:
   - Wild Goose Creative (September 2021) - two-day SOLD OUT gallery
   - Columbus Metropolitan Library Carnegie Gallery (April-June 2022)
   - Stonewall Columbus (June-July 2022)
   - Courtright Memorial Library at Otterbein

4. **Outstanding Reviews**: Emma Parker Photography, LLC has a 5-star rating with 96 reviews, demonstrating exceptional client satisfaction.
