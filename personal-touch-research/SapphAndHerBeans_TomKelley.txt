====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
Sappho and Her Beans is a Woman and LGBT owned coffee roasting operation based in Cleveland, Ohio. They focus on small batch, direct trade coffee from organic and fair trade sources. The business name "Sappho" references the ancient Greek poet from Lesbos, signaling LGBTQ+ identity and community focus. They currently have producing partners in Brazil, Ethiopia, and Nicaragua where they source direct trade coffee.

- Email marketing and customer retention for subscription/repeat purchases
- Social media content creation to showcase roasting process, origin stories, LGBTQ+ values
- SEO optimization for online coffee sales
- Packaging design and brand identity to stand out on shelves/online
