BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
========================================
Energetic Awakenings is an organizational development and wellness consulting firm located in North Ridgeville, OH (about 25 minutes from downtown Cleveland). Founded by David deBardelaben-Phillips, the company provides personal transformation through body-based therapy & energy work, and assists businesses with organizational development & change management.

The practice combines individual coaching & counseling (for anxiety, depression, trauma, grief, relationship problems, and self-esteem) with corporate consulting focused on challenging topics such as racism, conflict, and LGBTQ cultural competency.

Energetic Awakenings is a certified LGBT-owned business.
