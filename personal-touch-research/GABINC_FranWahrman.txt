========================================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
========================================
GAB Inc. is a global marketing and business services company that powers businesses with specialized marketing expertise transcending borders and industries. Founded by Cleveland native Gabby Gabriel on September 9, 2019, the company partners with brands of all sizes to help them break barriers, reach new audiences, and achieve measurable success.

GAB Inc. is the parent company for different subsidiaries including GAB China (specialty marketing agency), GAB Asia (marketing department in Southeast Asia), and Second Wind Media. The company has a presence in India, Pakistan, Thailand, and the Netherlands. Headquarters is in the Greater New York Area.

Website: thegabinc.com
