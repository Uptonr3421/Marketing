BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
Sits 'n Wiggles is Cuyahoga County's premier positive reinforcement dog training and behavior consulting company, founded by Valarie Ross, CDBC, CPDT-KA, CFFP (Certified Dog Behavior Consultant, Certified Professional Dog Trainer, Certified Fear Free Professional). Specializing in fear, anxiety, reactivity, and aggression in dogs since 2015, the company offers expert, evidence-backed, rewards-based training and behavior modification. Services include 1:1 behavior consulting, service dog training, day training, private coaching, and a 6-week puppy program. The business is queer, woman owned and operated, serving Cuyahoga County since 2015.
