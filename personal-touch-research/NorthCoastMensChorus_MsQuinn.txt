====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
North Coast Men's Chorus (NCMC) is Northeast Ohio's largest LGBT arts organization, founded in 1988 by Rick Fuller. The chorus has over 100 members and welcomes all men (cisgender and transgender) and non-binary tenors, baritones, and basses. Their primary mission is to "affirm the gay experience through music" and "touch the hearts and change the minds" of residents in the region through choral music performances.

Website: ncmchorus.org

RECENT WINS/NEWS/ACHIEVEMENTS
