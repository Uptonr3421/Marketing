====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
Talespinner Children's Theatre is Cleveland's dedicated professional theatre for young audiences, offering youth theatre performance and production opportunities, in-school residencies, professional performances, and more. Founded in 2011 by Alison (Ali) Garrigan as a 501(c)3 nonprofit, it emerged from a Cleveland Public Theatre artists' meeting when they identified the missing piece in Cleveland's theatre world: "a professional children's theater." Currently offers six professional theatrical productions each season: two for children ages 0-5, and four for ages 5+. Uses Pay-What-You-Choose model for all performances.

Location: 78th Street Studios, 1305 W 80th St, Ste 214, Cleveland, OH 44102
Founded: 2011
Founder: Alison (Ali) Garrigan
Model: Pay-What-You-Choose pricing
