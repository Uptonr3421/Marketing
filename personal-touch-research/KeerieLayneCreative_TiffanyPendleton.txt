=== BUSINESS DESCRIPTION ===
Keerie Layne Creative is a handmade gifts and decor business run by a designer and artist (Keerie Layne) who specializes in unique, custom gifts and decor for special events and occasions. The business creates lovingly crafted and customized products for all occasions.

Products & Services:
- Custom pressed flower bookmarks and mugs
- Custom drinkware and apparel
- Yard signs, invites, and decor for graduation parties
- Event decor (weddings, parties, celebrations)
- Handmade gifts for special occasions
- Customized products available at Northside Marketplace and Whiskers and Wine cat cafe

Owner Background:
- Originally from Massachusetts
- Ran an IT department for a school district for almost 10 years
- Pursued her creative passion during the pandemic
- Began creating decor for her own wedding during pandemic
- Transitioned from IT career to creative entrepreneurship
