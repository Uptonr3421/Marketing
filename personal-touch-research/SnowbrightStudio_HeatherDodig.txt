=== BUSINESS DESCRIPTION ===
Snowbright Studio is an LGBTQ+ game studio founded by Grace Collins that creates heartwarming games in welcoming and inclusive new worlds. The studio develops board games, card games, role-playing games, and tabletop games, as well as educational technology games. Snowbright is described as a games, research, and ed tech development studio.

Founder Grace Collins (they/them):
- Outspoken LGBTQ+ and gender equity advocate
- 2019 LGBTQIA+ Fellow for the Union for Reform Judaism
- Previously led game-based education policy for U.S. Department of Education
- Coordinated Federal Games Working Group across executive branch under Obama and Trump administrations
- Named in Reform Judaism magazine's article "Life Outside of the Binary"

Games & Products:
- Teatime Adventures
- ink (game)
- Time Tails RPG
- Harvest Hoppers
- Civics Bridge (educational game with IES grant)
- Companion app for various Snowbright games
- First educational game to feature canonically non-binary character (Time Tales)

Company Mission: Creating heartwarming games that spark imagination and inspire action in people of all ages, with focus on LGBTQ+ representation and inclusive worlds.
