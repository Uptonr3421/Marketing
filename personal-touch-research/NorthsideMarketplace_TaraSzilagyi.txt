====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
Northside Marketplace is an urban market with a focus on social interaction, local businesses, and grassroots artisans located in downtown Akron's Northside District. Like a retail incubator, Northside Marketplace identifies up-and-coming businesses and brings them together to create a unique local shopping experience. With support from the Knight Foundation and local business leaders, the marketplace offers small local businesses the tools and support they need to grow their brand and increase product exposure. Shop through products from over 35 vendors, hang out with friends, or watch games in the social lounge while enjoying culinary offerings like Nomz restaurant.

Location: 21 Furnace St, Akron, OH 44308
Hours: Closed Monday; Tue-Sat 11am-7pm; Sun 11am-3pm
Opened: Black Friday 2017
Developed by: Joel Testa and Testa Companies
