BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
========================================
Howling Print & Promo is an LGBT owned and operated print, promotional products, and apparel company based in Chardon, Ohio (Greater Cleveland Area). The company provides full range of promotional products and printing services to businesses across the United States.

In 2019, Howling Print and Promo was Certified as a LGBT Business Enterprise (LGBTBE) by the NGLCC (National LGBT Chamber of Commerce), opening doors to supplier diversity opportunities.

The owner, Ed Hamner, has discussed how LGBTBE certification's supplier diversity channels have led to increased business opportunities.

Website: howlingpromo.com
