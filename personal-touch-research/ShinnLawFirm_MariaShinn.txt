=== BUSINESS DESCRIPTION ===
The Shinn Law Firm, LLC is a Cleveland estate planning and probate law firm specializing in estate planning, probate and estate administration, elder law, and nontraditional family law. Founded in 2000 by Maria Shinn, the firm has helped hundreds of LGBTQ+ families with estate planning, adoptions, parentage actions, and probate. Maria graduated from Cleveland-Marshall Law School in 1997 magna cum laude and has 28 years of legal experience with a focus on serving the LGBTQ+ community and nontraditional families.
