===========================================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
===========================================
ALH Law Group is an LGBT Business Enterprise (LGBTBE) and Women's Business Enterprise (WBE) certified law firm founded by Allison L. Harrison in 2014. The firm provides litigation services and legal counsel for businesses and individuals across multiple states.

Practice areas include:
• Estate Planning & Probate - Specializing in LGBTQ+ community needs
• Business Law - Vendor contracts, lawsuits, consumer disputes, HR issues
• Consumer Law & Compliance - Retail lenders, consumer privacy, federal warranty law
• Litigation - Business disputes, consumer protection
• Financial Markets and Services

The firm has been helping small businesses with their legal needs for over 12 years.

===========================================
FOUNDER'S STORY & BACKGROUND
===========================================
Allison L. Harrison founded ALH Law Group in 2014 after earning her juris doctor degree from Capital University Law School in 2010. She is licensed in Ohio, Michigan, and Pennsylvania, as well as federal courts in Ohio and Michigan.

Allison is not just a lawyer but a community leader and advocate who has dedicated her career to supporting LGBTQ+ individuals and minority-owned businesses. She shares her legal expertise through public speaking, blogs, social media, and her podcast, helping individuals and small businesses understand their rights and legal options.
