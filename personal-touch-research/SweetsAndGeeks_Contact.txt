====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
Sweets and Geeks is a pop-culture haven for retro and modern memorabilia and candy founded in October 2020. The mission is to be valued as the neighborhood's favorite candy & game store by bringing nostalgic candy, soda & pop culture of yesteryear to customers of today. They offer international snacks, their own line of freeze-dried candy made in town and packaged by hand, craft sodas in 600+ varieties, and games/collectibles for anime, tabletop games, Magic, and Pokemon.

Locations:
- Original store: 342 E Smith Rd, Medina, OH 44256 (near Historic Medina Square)
- North Olmsted, Ohio
- Wooster, Ohio
- Sandusky Mall
Website: sweetsandgeeks.com
Founded: October 2020
