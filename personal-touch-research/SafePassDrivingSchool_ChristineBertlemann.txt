====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
Safe Pass Driving School serves teens and adults in the Cleveland area, growing into a highly rated driving school serving students from nearly fifty local high schools and colleges. Licensed by the Department of Public Safety through the Ohio Driver Training Program Office, Safe Pass is run by former university administrators Swinton and Kristen. The school offers behind-the-wheel and classroom training in a variety of driving techniques for adults and teens, with all classroom lessons in person. The staff all identify as LGBTQ+ adults, offering a safe space for northeast Ohio queer would-be drivers. They also offer a one-hour Highway Free(way)dom Course for licensed drivers who need confidence on highways.
