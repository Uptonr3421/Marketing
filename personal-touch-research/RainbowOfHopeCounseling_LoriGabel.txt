====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
Rainbow of Hope Counseling LLC is a specialized therapy practice run by Mary Wesley (MSW, LISW-S, LMSW), a clinical social worker who works exclusively with LGBTQIA+ youth and adults. The practice specializes in providing referral letters for those seeking Gender Affirming Services, with a mission to eliminate barriers to trauma-informed affirming therapy. Mary provides teletherapy appointments to individuals in Ohio and Michigan.

Services: Trauma-informed LGBTQIA+ therapy, Gender Affirming Services letters, teletherapy
Certifications: LGBTQIA+ Competent and Affirming Clinician (Safe Zone), Trauma-Informed Practices
Values: Kink-affirming, sex worker-affirming, poly-affirming
