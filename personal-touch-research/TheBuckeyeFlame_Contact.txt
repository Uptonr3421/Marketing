====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
The Buckeye Flame is Ohio's only LGBTQ+ newsroom, launched in June 2020 as a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization. It is a state-wide LGBTQ community-oriented online news website based in northern Ohio that updates regularly and distributes news recaps in a weekly "Spark" newsletter every Thursday. The mission is to amplify the voices of LGBTQ+ Ohioans to support community and civic empowerment through engaging content chronicling their experiences, triumphs, and struggles. Publishes minimum five stories weekly with rolling online content.

Editor: Dr. Ken Schneck (Sarah Pettit Memorial Award for National LGBTQ+ Journalist of the Year)
Founded: June 2020
Model: Nonprofit online newsroom with weekly newsletter
Website: thebuckeyeflame.com
Recognition: Report for America newsroom partner
