=== BUSINESS DESCRIPTION ===
JToth Art creates hyper-color photographic pop art featuring Cleveland's iconic architecture and landscape. Owner Jason Toth, 37, is a Beachwood native who transforms photographs of Cleveland into vibrant, colorful artwork by amplifying existing colors and creating abstract interpretations.

Artistic Process:
- Starts with original photography
- Uses digital tools to pull out and amplify colors that exist in photos
- Tones down other colors to create dramatic effect
- Creates "photographic pop art" of familiar Cleveland scenes

Products & Services:
- Fine art prints of Cleveland landmarks and scenes
- Custom photographic pop art
- Gallery representation at City Goods, Ohio City
- Work displayed in various Cleveland locations including Rocket Mortgage Fieldhouse
