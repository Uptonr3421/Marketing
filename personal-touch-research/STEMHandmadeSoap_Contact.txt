====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
STEM Handmade Soap was started by Dave and Steve in the basement of their Lakewood, Ohio home as a simple science experiment. Steve's degree in biochemistry led to creating the perfect bar of all-natural soap after 18 months of research and feedback. The business has grown from basement experiments to two retail locations. Products are scented with essential oils for aromatherapy experience with natural, skin-loving ingredients that are great for skin and environmentally friendly.

Locations:
- Flagship store in Lakewood, Ohio
- Van Aken District in Shaker Heights
Products: Handmade soap, lip balm, hand & body lotion with essential oils
Instagram: @stemsoaps
Website: stemsoaps.com
Values: Handcrafted, all-natural, plant-based, eco-conscious, skin-loving, cruelty-free
