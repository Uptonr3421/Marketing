=== BUSINESS DESCRIPTION ===
Lavender and Lace Event Rentals is a wedding and specialty event rental company situated in the heart of Cleveland, founded by Rachel in 2020. The business specializes in timeless heirloom rentals for weddings and events, offering a curated collection of modern, antique, rustic, and unique items.

Services & Inventory:
- Wedding rentals (ceremony and reception decor)
- Specialty event rentals
- Timeless heirloom pieces
- Modern, antique, rustic, and unique inventory
- Wide range of rental goods for diverse event aesthetics
- Showroom viewings by appointment

Location:
The showroom is inside the historic Twist Drill Factory building in Cleveland, accessible by appointment for clients to view and select rental items.
