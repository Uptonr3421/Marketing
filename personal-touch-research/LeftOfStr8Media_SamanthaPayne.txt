=== BUSINESS DESCRIPTION ===
Left of Str8 Media and Consulting is founded by Scott Fullerton, a longtime LGBTQ+ community advocate and entrepreneur. The business operates on two fronts:

1. **Left of Str8 Podcast Network** - Internet radio network for LGBTQ+ community and straight allies
2. **Marketing Consulting** - Exclusively for LGBTQ+ owned businesses and brands

Podcast Network Focus:
- Entertainment, Foodies, Books, Music, and Advocacy programming
- Interviews with LGBTQ+ Entrepreneurs & Creators
- Network started July 2015 (one week after Supreme Court granted Marriage Equality)
- Built network over 10 years to include multiple podcasts

Services:
