=== BUSINESS DESCRIPTION ===
India Ink Tattoo Company is Akron's ONLY Black, female, and queer-owned tattoo shop, owned by India Rush. The shop represents a groundbreaking achievement as the first Black female-owned tattoo shop in Akron and possibly all of Summit County. India Rush came from a background in graphic design before pursuing her passion for tattooing.

Specialties:
- Hyper-realistic tattoos
- Portrait tattoos (realistic)
- Black & Grey Realism
- Color Realism
- Florals
- Fine-line pieces full of life, depth, and emotion

Unique Atmosphere:
Unlike traditional dark, edgy tattoo parlors, India's shop features natural light flooding through tall windows, creating a welcoming, warm, and relaxing environment. The space is intentionally designed to be inclusive and comfortable for all clients.
