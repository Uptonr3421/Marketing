=== BUSINESS DESCRIPTION ===
INNATE ELECTRIC LLC is an LGBTQ+-owned electrical contracting company founded in 2021 by Chelsea Brennan, a transgender woman and advocate. The company provides electrical contractor services for residential, commercial, and industrial clients across Northeast Ohio.

Owner Chelsea Brennan brings over 38 years of hands-on experience in the electrical trade, with vocational training in electronics, collegiate study in electrical engineering, and certified electrical apprenticeship credentials.

Company Mission: The main company goal is to hire LGBTQ+ individuals and provide a safe and affirming work environment while delivering exceptional electrical services.
