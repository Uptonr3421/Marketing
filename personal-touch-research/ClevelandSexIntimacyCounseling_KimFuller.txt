BUSINESS DESCRIPTION:
Cleveland Sex & Intimacy Counseling (CSIC) is a therapy practice providing individual, sex, and relationship therapy from a culturally-sensitive, sex-positive perspective. The practice was founded in 2020 by Dr. Kimberly Fuller, who serves as owner and lead therapist. CSIC specializes in empowering and supporting LGBTQ+, kink, and polyamorous communities, with clinicians trained to unpack relational and sexual concerns in an open-minded environment.

FOUNDER BACKGROUND:
Dr. Kimberly Fuller holds a PhD, MSW, MEd, LISW-S, and is an AASECT-certified sex therapist (CST). She is also an Associate Professor and BSW Program Director at Cleveland State University in the School of Social Work. Dr. Fuller has been providing sexuality trainings throughout the greater Cleveland area since 2016 when she began her role at Cleveland State.
