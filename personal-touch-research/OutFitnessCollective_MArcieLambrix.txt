====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
Out Fitness Collective is a grassroots LGBTQ+ fitness space offering affordable, private workout options in Cleveland's Detroit Shoreway neighborhood. Founded by Josh Jones Forbes, the facility provides a safe, welcoming environment for LGBTQ+ community members and allies to exercise on their own terms. Members can book private workout slots or train with Forbes as a personal trainer. The collective uses a flexible "pay what you can" pricing model to ensure accessibility.

Location: 7405 Detroit Ave, Cleveland (Gordon Square Arts District, blocks from LGBT Community Center)
Opened: Late 2022/Early 2023
Members: 75+ current members, 200+ people have worked out since opening
