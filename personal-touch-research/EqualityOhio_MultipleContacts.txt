========================================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
========================================
Equality Ohio is Ohio's statewide LGBTQ+ advocacy organization working to achieve equality and freedom for all lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, intersex, and asexual people in Ohio. The organization conducts advocacy, education, and legal services to advance LGBTQ+ equality across Ohio.

Equality Ohio has been operating for 20 years (marking 20th anniversary in 2025), working on legislative advocacy, legal support, community education, and policy change.

========================================
RECENT WINS/NEWS/ACHIEVEMENTS
========================================

1. **20th Anniversary (2025)**: Equality Ohio marked 20 years of LGBTQ+ advocacy in Ohio, celebrating two decades of fighting for equality despite ongoing challenges.

2. **20th Anniversary Gala (October 17, 2025)**: Hosted major fundraising gala celebrating 20 years of advocacy, bringing together supporters and raising visibility.

3. **Legal Clinic Expansion**: The statewide legal clinic has served more than 1,000 LGBTQ+ families and experienced 200% increase in client intakes since the presidential election - demonstrating urgent community need.

4. **Bold New Vision Announcement (October 2025)**: Despite organizational restructuring and staff layoffs, announced commitment to continuing advocacy with renewed vision.

5. **Crisis Response Leadership**: Responded to multiple legislative attacks in 2025 including HB 96 (anti-LGBTQ+ state budget proposal), Supreme Court ruling in U.S. v. Skrmetti, SB 104 (bathroom ban), and HB 8 (forced outing law).

========================================
RECENT CHALLENGES
========================================

**Major Organizational Restructuring (October 31, 2025)**: Equality Ohio announced dramatic staff reductions, eliminating positions including director of advocacy, communications manager, entire regional organizer team (three staff members covering Northeast Ohio, Central Ohio, and Mahoning Valley), and at least one attorney in the legal clinic.

This restructuring came less than a week after the mandatory 20th Anniversary Gala, creating organizational uncertainty but also demonstrating resilience and commitment to continuing mission.
