BUSINESS DESCRIPTION:
Cloak & Dagger is a craft cocktail bar and vegan restaurant in Cleveland's Tremont neighborhood. Opened in October 2020 by partners Casey Hughes and Cory Hajde, the bar features a book-themed environment designed to resemble a retired author's study, with leather-bound books, velvet seating, and writer's desk. All drinks and food are 100% vegan. Each seasonal menu is themed to a book or author.

FOUNDER BACKGROUND:
Casey Hughes (also listed as Casey Gerber) is co-owner and one of the lead bartenders. She curates the bar's creative vegan cocktails, drawing inspiration from "making food into cocktails" with unusual flavor combinations. She partnered with Cory Hajde to open the business. Chef Todd Kronika creates the vegan comfort food menu.
