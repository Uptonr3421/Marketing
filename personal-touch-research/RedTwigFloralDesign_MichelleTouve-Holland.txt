=== BUSINESS DESCRIPTION ===
The Red Twig is a luxury floral design studio and art gallery centrally located in Hudson, between Cleveland and Akron. Owner Michelle Touve-Holland has been surprising clients with her unusual, lively approach to floral design for nearly 25 years. Unlike standard florists with fixed inventory, The Red Twig takes an unrestrained approach to floral design, purchasing flowers exclusively for each client and using multiple techniques to tailor-make each event. The studio also operates an art gallery featuring local and national artists.
