=== BUSINESS DESCRIPTION ===
Jilly's Music Room is a live music and entertainment venue located in the Northside District of downtown Akron, directly across from the Downtown Akron Courtyard Marriott. The venue features original bands (local, regional, and national) in a variety of genres and hosts live entertainment at least four nights a week.

Venue Specialties:
- Live music performances (Blues, R&B, Rock, Indie, Pop, Singer/Songwriter, Americana, Alt-Country, Jazz)
- Stand-up comedy shows
- Bar and kitchen service
- Private events and concerts
- Capacity: 225 people

Notable Partnerships:
- Akron Symphony Orchestra events (Broadway Karaoke Experience)
- Live Music Society member venue
- Independent Venue Week participant
