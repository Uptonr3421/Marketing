========================================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
========================================
Engage! Cleveland is a nonprofit organization dedicated to attracting, engaging, and retaining emerging, diverse talent to Greater Cleveland. The organization connects up-and-coming talent, employers, and civic and non-profit organizations, helping young professionals and emerging leaders connect with opportunities for professional development, networking, civic events, and social activities.

Engage! Cleveland works with over 115+ employer members to make Cleveland a lasting home for the next generation of talent, enhancing the vitality of Cleveland and surrounding communities by creating a strong community of young professionals and emerging leaders.

PAIN POINTS
========================================

1. **Competing with Major Cities**: Cleveland competes with Columbus, Chicago, NYC for young talent. Need compelling marketing and storytelling to position Cleveland as exciting, viable option for emerging professionals.

2. **Sustained Member Engagement**: With 115+ employer members, keeping all partners actively engaged and seeing ROI from membership requires ongoing communication, events, and value demonstration.

3. **Young Professional Awareness**: Many young professionals new to Cleveland may not know Engage! Cleveland exists. Requires consistent marketing, social media presence, and partnerships with universities/employers for visibility.

4. **Event Attendance & Registration**: Filling Young Professionals Week events and other programming requires effective promotion, compelling programming, and removing barriers to participation.

5. **Measuring Impact**: Demonstrating concrete retention numbers and showing how Engage! Cleveland activities translate to young professionals staying in Cleveland long-term.
