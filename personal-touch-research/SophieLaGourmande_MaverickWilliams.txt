====================
BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
====================
Sophie La Gourmande is a modern French-inspired patisserie and neighborhood café in the Cedar Lee Arts District of Cleveland Heights, highlighting seasonal menus and passion for French technique. Opened in May 2022, the business is owned by veteran chef, caterer, and culinary instructor Bob Sferra, who spent years (since 2019 acquisition) transforming a 1930s-era building on Lee Road. The café specializes in fancy French pâtisserie and viennoiserie including croissants, Kouign Sophie (signature item), everything Danish with labneh cream cheese, and mini Basque cheesecake. Everything is made from scratch in the spotless open kitchen by talented, dedicated chefs and pastry chefs.

Location: 2275/2277 Lee Rd, Cleveland Heights, OH 44118 (Cedar Lee Arts District)
