## The Psychology: Why This Works

### 1. Reciprocity (42% Higher Response)
**What:** Give value first, ask later
**How:** Touch 1 provides insight/resource with no ask
**Result:** People feel compelled to reciprocate

### 2. Specificity (73% Trust Increase)
**What:** Reference specific details about their company
**How:** Every email mentions specific achievement/project/person
**Result:** Builds credibility and trust instantly

### 3. Multi-Touch (80% of Sales Happen Touches 5-12)
**What:** Systematic touchpoint sequence
**How:** 12-touch campaign with varied content
**Result:** Don't give up too early like 80% of salespeople

### 4. Social Proof (34% Conversion Increase)
**What:** Reference similar organizations you've helped
**How:** "3 museums similar to yours saw 47-89% increases..."
**Result:** Reduces perceived risk

### 5. Scarcity + Urgency
**What:** Limited spots, specific timeframes
**How:** "Working with 3 organizations this quarter, one spot opened..."
**Result:** Drives action
