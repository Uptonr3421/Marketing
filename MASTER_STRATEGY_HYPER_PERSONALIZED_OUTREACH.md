### Email Body Frameworks

**1. PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution)**
- **Problem**: Clearly state their pain point
- **Agitation**: Elaborate on negative consequences
- **Solution**: Your offering as relief

**2. Three R's (Research-Reference-Reward)**
- **Research**: Show you did homework (specific observation)
- **Reference**: Mention similar customer success
- **Reward**: Value proposition

**3. Mouse Trap (1-2 sentences only)**
- **Observation**: Context for why you're reaching out
- **Question**: Value-prop driven, implies solution

**Length:**
- **75-125 words = sweet spot**
- Keep concise, scannable
- Use "you" not "we"
- Break into short paragraphs
